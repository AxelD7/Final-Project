About The Project

We are a website that helps kids or students that travel a lot keep up with their home/public school work. Having to move around a lot or be in a car while trying to do work can be difficult. Bumpy roads can cause mistakes or make all your tools to learn fall to the ground. It can be frustrating and demotivating to constantly be disrupted while working. We give you access to sections for note taking and calculation that you can carry with you all the time. Give us a shot and see how you like it.

How to use our tools

Notes

This one is pretty simple, just put any text you'd like to remember for later. (Tip: If you are taking notes on a video you can drag both tabs to the farthest side on your screen to evenly split the pages up.)

Calculator

  1. Input your first operand.
  2. Choose the Operation you would like to use.
  3. Input your second operand.
  4. Press equals and get your results.
